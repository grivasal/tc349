About Object Manipulation

Object manipulation is a form of dexterity play or performance in which one or more people physically interact with one or more objects. Object manipulation skills are often recognized as circus skills.

Many forms of object manipulation have developed from various traditional and cultural skills.

There are a very wide range of types of object manipulation, including poi spinning, juggling, contact juggling, hula hooping, spinning staffs, and many others.

This site gives a brief introduction, with associated images, into a few popular forms of object manipulation.
